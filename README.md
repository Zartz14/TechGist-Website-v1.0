# TechGist – UQUCC Newsletter Archive

TechGist is a website created to archive and organize newsletters published by the **Umm Al-Qura University Computer Club (UQUCC)**.
The platform provides a simple and accessible way for students to explore previous newsletters, announcements, and technology-related insights shared by the club.

---

## 🌐 Live Website

https://techgist.webflow.io/

---

## 📌 Project Overview

TechGist was developed as a centralized platform where the newsletters produced by UQUCC can be stored, browsed, and accessed easily by students and club members.

The goal of the project is to preserve valuable content produced by the club and make it easily discoverable for both current and future students.

---

## ⚠️ Problem

Before TechGist, newsletters and announcements were often shared across different platforms such as messaging groups or social media. Over time this caused several issues:

* Past newsletters became difficult to find.
* Important information and insights were easily lost.
* New members had no clear way to explore previous content.
* There was no centralized archive for the club’s knowledge and updates.

---

## 💡 Solution

TechGist solves this by providing a **central archive website** where all newsletters can be stored and accessed in one place.

The platform allows students to:

* Browse previous newsletter issues
* Access content in a clean reading interface
* Navigate between issues easily
* Discover older content produced by the club

This creates a **long-term knowledge archive** for UQUCC.
